Conclusion

Although many theories exist on the disappearance of the colonists, we have discussed three popular theories.

John White's Search

A few pieces of information that continue to puzzle theorists is the idea that John White had not searched more for the colonists, especially since his family was part of the colonists he left behind.

Accounts Verbally Communicated

Additionally, the accounts of natives killing colonists or descending from colonists are difficult to believe because they are verbal and mostly inferences concluded by the people who documented the accounts.

Many possible fates

It is possible that the colonists died from starvation or drought, since White had left the colonists in order to acquire more supplies. However, the most likely explanation for the disapperance of the colonists based on the evidence is assimilation into a Native Tribe.
