Natives killed Colonists

Another theory considers the anger of local Native American tribes against the Europeans taking their land. They could have written “CROATOAN” in regards to the Croatan natives that lived nearby and didn’t have good relations with the colonists.

Colonists carve "CROATOAN" As Warning

Remaining colonists could have carved the cryptic message in an attempt to communicate to anyone who would go back to the settlement to investigate the disappearances.

No Bodies Found

One large piece of evidence that disproves this theory is the fact that there were no bodies found near the settlement.
