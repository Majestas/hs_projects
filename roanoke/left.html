Colonists Possibly Moved

A third theory describes how the colonists may have moved to a separate location. When John White returned to Roanoke, the word “Croatoan” was written on a post at the location of the colony.

Unable to look for Colonists

White assumed that the sign meant that the colonists had moved to the nearby Croatoan Island, which is currently known as Hatteras Island. Due to a storm, White and his crew were unable to search Croatoan Island, so it is unclear as to whether or not the colonists were actually on Croatoan Island.

Did not reach Croaton Island

Years later, another search party confirmed that the colonists did not make it to the other island. Recently, there has been evidence that there might have been an emergency fort on the mainland that had been covered up in the map in case any enemies got hold of it.

Possibly Moved Somewhere Else

Although it is unclear as to where the colonists may have moved, many theorists believe the colonists found another area to live that was more suitable during the drought period.
