Raleigh Lands

After Lane abandoned the settlement, Raleigh recruited 117 men, women and children to start a settlement more permanent than the last and appointed John White the governor.

Returns for Supplies

Going back for supplies, Fernandez (Captain of The Lion, ship that carried the colonists) left Roanoke only to be trapped in England by an invasion of the Spanish Armada.

White's Return

White returned to the islands with supples to find that the town had been deserted and vacant for a while with only one clue as to where everyone disappeared to: "CROATOAN".
