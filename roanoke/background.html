England wishes to Colonize

In the late 16th Century, Queen Elizabeth attempted to set up colonies in the United States. April 27, 1584, Sir Walter Raleigh left with a party of 100 soldiers, miners and scientists to Roanoke where they explored the islands.

Settlers Arrive...a little late

Known as the Lost Colony, Roanoke Island is in what is now Dare County, North Carolina. When Raleigh and the settlers got there, they were too late in the season for planting and with their supplies decreasing by the day, they did not have chance against the land.

Sir Francis Drake Arrives

After Sir Francis Drake landed in Roanoke in 1586 because of a failed expedition, Lane and his soldiers abandoned the settlement, leaving behind their fort.
